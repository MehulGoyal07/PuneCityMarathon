1. Event Overview:

1.1. The Pune City Marathon is a long-distance running event 3Km, 5Km, 10Km and Half Marathon.

1.2. The Event is scheduled to take place on 2nd Feb 2025 in Poona Club, Pune, India.

2. Participant Registration:

2.1. Participation in the Event is open to individuals of all ages, provided they meet the eligibility criteria and registration requirements set by the Organizer.

2.2. Participants must register for the Event online through the official Event website www.punecitymarathon.com
