Success Story

DONATIONS MADE THROUGH OUR BENEFICIARIY ORGANISATIONS

  • 100 walkers, 100 walking Sticks, 100 Wheel chairs to our senior citizens.
  • 300 school bags and school books to the underprivileged students of the society in Sant Ghadhge Maharaj School (Koregaon Park).
  • 55 braille kits to the blind students with special needs.
  • Hearing aid kits to the hearing Impaired citizens.
  • An Initiative for the Betterment of Society: Our next philanthropic endeavour is tree planting initiative which will be executed in the Army area.

To sum up, all this would have not been possible without our sponsors whose whole heartedly supported us with confidence in our team.

Special Thanks to

  • Helios for providing the post race physiotherapist.
  • Oxyrich for giving on route hydration support.
  • Ruby hall clinic for taking care of the medical assistance during the race.
  • Chitale Bandhu for arranging breakfast for all the participants.
  • Kaveri Caterers for organizing breakfast for all sponsors and our VIP dignitaries
  • Adar Poonawala Clean City for helping us clean up the entire route pre and post race.
  • PMC for providing sanitation on route
  • Pune police for helping us control traffic during the race
