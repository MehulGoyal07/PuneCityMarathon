10Km Empower Run

The 10KM Empower Run is a thrilling challenge designed for amateur and seasoned runners alike. This category emphasizes self-discipline, endurance, and empowerment, making it a significant milestone for those looking to push their limits. It’s an opportunity to test your stamina, compete against the clock, and experience the joy of achieving a personal best.

Inclusions in the Registration Fee

Participants of the 10KM Empower Run will receive:

  • Unisex Dry Fit T-Shirt: A high-quality, event-branded T-shirt for maximum comfort and style.
  • Finisher Medal: A medal symbolizing your accomplishment and dedication.
  • Goody Bag: Loaded with exciting souvenirs and items for your running journey.
  • Breakfast: A post-run meal to replenish your energy.
  • Bib: Your official bib for race identification.
  • Timing Chip: A timing chip for accurate tracking of your performance.
  • Hydration on Route: Water and electrolyte stations placed strategically to keep you energized.
  • En-Route Medical Support: Comprehensive medical assistance throughout the route to ensure a safe experience.
