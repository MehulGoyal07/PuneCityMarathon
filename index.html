About Us

Pune City Marathon is more than just a race—it’s a mission to inspire fitness, foster inclusivity, and champion environmental and social causes. Our vision is to bring together people from all walks of life to promote health, sustainability, and community.

Since its inception, the Pune City Marathon has become a beacon of hope, determination, and unity. It is a celebration of the city's vibrant spirit, where every participant takes a step toward creating a greener and more compassionate Pune.

Whether you're an avid runner, a fitness enthusiast, or someone looking to contribute to a meaningful cause, the Pune City Marathon has something for everyone.

Event Overview

  • Diverse Distance Categories

    Our marathon welcomes participants of all fitness levels, offering distances of 3km, 5km, 10km, and 21.1km. Every step symbolizes a commitment to personal health and the preservation of our environment.

  • Eco-Friendly Initiatives

    Staying true to our pledge for sustainability, the Pune City Marathon actively incorporates eco-friendly practices:

    • Minimal use of single-use plastics.
    • Compostable materials for refreshments.
    • Waste segregation and zero-waste stations throughout the marathon route.

  • Social Impact

    Through partnerships with NGOs, government bodies, and corporate sponsors, the marathon supports transformative initiatives, including:

    • Clean City Green City campaigns.
    • "You Run, We Plant" tree plantation drives.
    • Supporting the education and welfare of children of marginalized workers.
    • Empowering the visually and hearing impaired.
    • Supporting old-age homes.
